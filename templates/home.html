Mission

Glen Park Parent Teacher Organization (GPPTO) is a nonprofit parent teacher organization. The PTO's mission is to promote understanding between parents, teachers, students and the greater community, and to work together to make Glen Park Elementary the best it can be.

Get Involved!

When parents get connected to the school, children do better. Thousands of research studies show that children whose parents are involved get better grades, do better on tests, and have fewer discipline problems at school. Just a few hours can make a tremendous difference!

Whether you have one day a month or one day a week, volunteering your time at Glen Park Elementary makes a world of difference in our students’ lives. As part of the Glen Park School community, you are automatically a member of the Glen Park Parent Teacher Organization. The PTO is the vehicle that lets parents really get involved at Glen Park School. By necessity, the PTO has begun to raise funds to continue many of our great programs. But we are also about building community and family involvement at the school.

In the Classroom

Ask your teacher about working in the classroom. Parents are needed to help with prep work, read to the class, with art projects and lots more! Being a chaperone on a field trip is also a great help to your teacher and a fun way to get to know your child's classmates.

Around the School

Volunteers are always needed all round the school. Help on the yard at lunch and recess. We"ll train you and give you the resources you need to make sure play time is fun and safe for all our students. Volunteering in the library can mean restacking the books, setting up a thematic display or reading to a class.

At an Event

Many of Glen Park’s wonderful events simply could not be pulled off without the help of PTO volunteers. People are always needed to help setup and clean-up, sell food, paint faces. There is a job for everyone and the list goes on and on. Volunteering at an event is a great way to meet other Glen Park families and is always a lot of fun.
